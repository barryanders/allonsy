Privacy Policy

Simple, transparent handling of your data.

What Z-Templates collects

Z-Templates does not collect or transmit email content. Templates, styles, and settings are stored locally in your browser.

Payments and licensing

Billing and licensing are handled by ExtensionPay, which may process your account email and payment details via Stripe. Z-Templates does not receive or store full payment information.

How data is used

  • To save templates and settings locally.
  • To verify trial or lifetime access through ExtensionPay.

Data sharing

No data is sold. Z-Templates does not use analytics, ads, or tracking.
